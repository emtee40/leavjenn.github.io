Hews 2 Notification Account Privacy FAQ

Is this my Hacker News account?

NO. The notification account has nothing to do with your Hacker News account. This account is only related to the Hews 2 app.

Why do I need another account?

To get push notifications of new comments and sync related data across devices.

Why only provide social sign in, no email sign up?

Because it's, well, easier. I don't need to work on features like verifying email address, forgetting password, resetting password, etc.

More detail?

The Hews 2 notification system is totally based on Firebase services.

Firebase Auth for account management; Firebase realtime database for storing notification-related data; Firebase cloud function for checking Hacker News update and sending notification via Firebase cloud messaging.

Your sign-in social account-associated email.

Yes.
